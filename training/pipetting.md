Pipetting
---------
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
Description:

This protocol teaches the fundementals of pipetting though a large and a small 
volume pipetting exercise and then demonstrates how to calculate the error
associated with pipetting.
